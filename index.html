About Us

We are launching a modern agricultural service that uses drones for crop spraying and precision farming. Our mission is to help farmers adopt modern technology to improve crop productivity, reduce labor costs, and ensure accurate pesticide application.

The project will initially start in Western Rajasthan, particularly in the districts of Jodhpur and Nagaur, then gradually expanded to entire western rajasthan including Barmer and Phalodi. . The pilot phase will focus on crops such as Bt Cotton and Cumin (Jeera), which require frequent spraying and careful chemical management.

Drone spraying technology allows uniform chemical distribution, reduces farmer exposure to harmful pesticides, and saves time and water.

Pesticide Spraying

Pesticide spraying is an essential agricultural activity used to protect crops from insects, diseases, and weeds. Traditional spraying methods using manual pumps or tractors are often time-consuming and inefficient.

  • Control insects and pests
  • Protect crops from fungal diseases
  • Improve crop yield
  • Reduce crop damage
  • Ensure uniform chemical application

Drone-based spraying significantly improves efficiency by covering large agricultural areas quickly and precisely.

Drone Spraying

Agricultural drones are unmanned aerial vehicles designed for precision farming. These drones can spray pesticides, fertilizers, and nutrients over crops efficiently.

  • Spray 10–20 acres per hour
  • Uniform pesticide distribution
  • Reduced chemical wastage
  • Minimal water usage
  • Reduced human exposure to chemicals
  • Ideal for cotton, cumin, wheat, and mustard crops

Drone spraying is becoming an important part of modern agriculture and is being supported by government initiatives and agricultural innovation programs.
